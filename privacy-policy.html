2. Scope

This policy applies to all Chrome extensions published by Nicolas Follezou on the Chrome Web Store, including OneClic and EasyRec. Each extension is described separately where their data practices differ.

3. Data We Collect — and What We Do Not

3.1 OneClic (Tab Workflow & Session Saver)

We do not collect any personal data. OneClic does not use tracking, analytics, or telemetry. All your workflows (tab names, URLs, titles, favicons) and preferences are stored exclusively on your device using chrome.storage.local and never transmitted to any server.

3.2 EasyRec (Camera Recorder)

EasyRec stores recording preferences locally on your device via chrome.storage.local. Recordings (video, screen captures, photos) are processed entirely in your browser and saved locally via chrome.downloads. No recording content is ever uploaded to any server.

EasyRec transmits anonymous, non-identifiable usage events (e.g. "recording started", "video exported") to Google Analytics 4 via the Measurement Protocol. These events contain no name, email address, IP address, or device identifier. The sole purpose is to understand feature usage and improve the extension.

5. Third-Party Services

Both extensions offer an optional paid Pro upgrade, processed by:

  • ExtensionPay — manages license activation and verification. Acts as a data processor on our behalf.
  • Stripe, Inc. — processes payments. Your email address and payment information are collected and processed directly by Stripe under their own Privacy Policy. Stripe is based in the United States; transfers are covered by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) under GDPR Art. 46.

EasyRec additionally uses:

  • Google Analytics 4 (Google LLC) — receives anonymous usage events. Google is based in the United States; transfers are covered by SCCs. No PII is transmitted. You may opt out of analytics by disabling the extension's analytics setting if available.

6. Data Retention

  • Local extension data (workflows, settings, recordings): retained on your device until you uninstall the extension or clear its storage.
  • Payment data: retained by Stripe and ExtensionPay according to their own policies. We do not store payment card data.
  • Anonymous analytics: retained by Google Analytics for 14 months, then automatically deleted.
